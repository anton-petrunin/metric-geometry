\title{Pure metric geometry:\\
introductory lectures}
\author{Anton Petrunin}
\date{}
\maketitle

We discuss only domestic affairs of metric spaces, leaving all  	external applications aside.

This is the introductory part to the course in metric geometry given at Penn State, Spring 2020.
A part of the text is a compilation from \cite{alexander-kapovitch-petrunin-2019, alexander-kapovitch-petrunin-2025, petrunin-yashinski, petrunin-2009, petrunin-zamorabarrera} and its drafts.
The complete lectures can be found on the authors website;
it includes an introduction to Alexandrov geometry based on \cite{alexander-kapovitch-petrunin-2019} and metric geometry on manifolds \cite{petrunin2020} based on a simplified of proof Gromov's systolic inequality given by Alexander Nabutovsky~\cite{nabutovsky}.
