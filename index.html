METRIC GEOMETRY AND COMPARISON (MATH 597, section 2)
(Geometry of metric spaces including different types
of convergence.Introduction to Alexandrov geometry.
Applications in group theory, and topology.)

  • An invitation to Alexandrov geometry: CAT(0) spaces by S. Alexander, V. Kapovitch and A. Petrunin.
  • Metric structures for Riemannian and non-Riemannian spaces by M. Gromov.
